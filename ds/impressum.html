Business Model & Operational Scope

We are a pure B2B electronics wholesale and logistics partner. Our business model is exclusively B2B – we supply authorized retailers and distributors within a closed partner network. We do not operate an online shop, do not sell to private consumers, and do not engage in public advertising or marketing campaigns.

Incoterms: All deliveries are executed under DDP (Delivered Duty Paid) Incoterms 2020 via our authorized German logistics partner DHL. We assume full responsibility for the entire goods flow from the factory, including customs clearance, taxes, and comprehensive cargo insurance, up to the buyer's delivery address.

Product Warranty: We distribute exclusively brand-new, original packaged products from leading manufacturers through authorized distribution channels. All products are CE-certified, comply with EU specifications, and are configured for the respective target country. The manufacturer's warranty can be claimed locally at any authorized specialist dealer in Europe.

Company History & Financial Development

2017: Founded on 26 December 2017 in Thailand as a retail company.

2018: Transition to wholesale and distribution, becoming a full-service logistics partner for electronics manufacturers and retailers, focusing on the European market.

2019: Expansion of the partner network and establishment of the European Partner Desk. First DDP contracts with DHL for direct shipping to Germany, Austria, and Switzerland.

2020: Achieved ISO 9001 and ISO 14001 certifications. Logistics center in Bangkok expanded.

2021–2022 (COVID-19 Pandemic): Operations were restricted to approximately 8–10% of normal capacity due to government-mandated lockdowns, closed ports, and temporary import bans in various EU countries. Revenue in these years was significantly reduced (approx. 770,000 EUR in 2020). These figures are pandemic outliers and do not reflect the company's normal performance.

2023: Full recovery and strong growth. Partner network expanded to 921 companies across 7 EU countries. Revenue exceeded 50 million USD.

2024–2025: Achieved additional certifications: ISO 27001 (Information Security), ISO 45001 (Occupational Health), SA8000 (Social Accountability), and AEO Status (Customs Security). Recognized with multiple awards: Best Electronics Exporter 2024 (Thai Chamber of Commerce), Top SME Digital Transformation 2023 (Ministry of Digital Economy), Outstanding Business Partner 2022 (German-Thai Business Association).

2026: Continued growth. The company operates with 82 employees, 921 partners, and an annual revenue of 50–94 million USD. The EDI gateway and European partner network are fully operational.

Note: All financial figures from 2020–2021 are pandemic outliers and do not represent the company's current performance.
