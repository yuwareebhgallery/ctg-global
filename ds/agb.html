These General Terms and Conditions apply to all business relationships between Chumpol Trading Group Company Limited (hereinafter "CTG" or "Seller") and its business customers (hereinafter "Buyer"). 2. Conclusion of Contract
All offers by CTG are subject to change and non-binding unless expressly designated as binding. The contract is only concluded upon CTG's written order confirmation or the actual delivery of the ordered goods to the Buyer.

3. Delivery and Services
CTG delivers exclusively under Incoterms® DDP (Delivered Duty Paid) to the delivery address specified by the Buyer. This means that CTG assumes full responsibility for the entire goods flow from the factory, including all customs formalities, taxes, and comprehensive goods insurance, up to the Buyer's delivery address. Deliveries are made ex works or from the manufacturer's warehouse. Dropshipping is available upon request.

4. Prices and Payment Terms
All prices are ex works or ex manufacturer's warehouse plus statutory VAT, unless DDP has been expressly agreed. Prices are exclusive of packaging, shipping, customs, and insurance costs, which will be invoiced separately. The invoice amount is due for payment within 14 days of the invoice date without deduction. In the event of default, CTG is entitled to charge default interest at a rate of 9 percentage points above the respective base interest rate.

5. Retention of Title
CTG retains title to all delivered goods until full payment of all claims arising from the ongoing business relationship (extended retention of title). The Buyer is entitled to resell the reserved goods in the ordinary course of business as long as it is not in default with its payment obligations to CTG.

6. Warranty
For defects in the delivered goods, CTG's liability to entrepreneurs is as follows: The Buyer must inspect the delivered goods immediately upon receipt and notify CTG in writing of obvious defects within 14 days of receipt. The manufacturer's warranty remains unaffected.

CTG shall be fully liable for damages resulting from injury to life, body, or health caused by an intentional or grossly negligent breach of duty.
