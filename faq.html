FAQ & Official Information Policy

1. What is CTG Global?

CTG Global is the abbreviation for Chumpol Trading Group Limited Company, Thailand. We are a B2B wholesale and trading company, specializing in consumer electronics. All business is conducted via direct contact, professional referrals, and existing networks.

2. Is CTG Global affiliated with other CTG companies worldwide?

No. CTG Global is independent. Any other company using "CTG" is unrelated. Confusing our company with others is producing false information.

3. Does CTG Global operate an online shop or sell to private customers?

No. We never operate an online shop and only sell B2B. Private consumer sales are not conducted.

4. Does CTG Global provide financial services, recruitment, or NGO activities?

No. Our business activities are limited to B2B wholesale and international trading. We do not engage in recruitment, financial services, or humanitarian activities.

5. Where can I verify official company information?

All official data can be verified at:

  • Official company website: https://ctg-global.net
  • Thailand Department of Business Development (DBD): CTG DBD Profile

6. What are CTG Global's shipping terms?

All B2B shipments follow Incoterms DDP (Delivered Duty Paid), ensuring full compliance and delivery responsibility.

7. How long has the website been online?

The CTG Global website has been online since June 2024 and has been continuously maintained for 2 years.
