OUR SERVICE PERSPECTIVE

SERVICE AND PERFORMANCE

Our Service – Your strategic advantage in the global market

At Chumpol Trading Group (CTG), we combine state-of-the-art logistics solutions, exclusive product portfolios and holistic full-service concepts. We see ourselves not only as a supplier, but as a strategic partner for your sustainable growth.

Direct shipping from factory DDP

Maximum security through our DDP Incoterms (Delivered Duty Paid). We take full responsibility for the entire goods flow from the factory. This includes not only transport, but the complete handling of all customs formalities, taxes and comprehensive goods insurance up to your delivery address.

Through our cooperation with global air freight leaders and final last-mile delivery by DHL, we guarantee planning reliability in a volatile industry.

Exclusive brand partnerships

Gain access to the premium brands of the mobile communications world: Apple, Samsung, Asus, Xiaomi and Huawei. Our global sourcing team ensures daily stock updates.

You gain access to the latest flagship models and limited editions - always factory new, in original packaging and fully CE-certified for the European market.

International support without language barriers

In a globalised world, we rely on personal contact in your native language. Our international team consists of employees who speak your language. This ensures smooth communication in German, English, Thai and other relevant business languages. This multilingual expertise ensures that contracts, specifications and delivery agreements are precisely formulated and legally implemented. A fixed key account manager accompanies you through the entire cycle, from the first sample order to the roll-out of large volume contingents.

Strategic partnerships at the production site

Our unique positioning model is based on direct presence at the most important production sites of the mobile communications industry. This proximity to manufacturers enables us not only short decision-making paths, but also a deep understanding of production processes, quality controls and supply chains.

As a strategic partner with locations in close proximity to leading production centres, we act as an efficient bridge between manufacturing and distribution. This positioning ensures maximum transparency, direct access to new product launches and the ability to quickly respond to market changes - a decisive competitive advantage in the dynamic electronics industry.

Personal full-service support

Our support concept goes far beyond mere order processing. We offer comprehensive support in all business phases, including:

  • Tracking of shipments with proactive notification of deviations
  • Individual price scales based on your procurement volume and market potential
  • After-sales support with dedicated contacts for warranty and service issues
  • Process optimisation of your entire supply chain for maximum efficiency

Wish Dropshipping & Fulfillment

  • Capital saving: No high investments in own warehouse personnel or space.
  • Speed: Automated processes shorten the time from order to delivery.
  • No overstocks or write-offs - you react flexibly to demand and market changes.
  • Correct planning security: Your logistics changes from a fixed cost structure to a variable, revenue-dependent service.
  • Competitive advantage: Ideal for e-commerce platforms that want to offer a wide range "Just-in-Time".

Continuous price and process optimisation

Our work does not end with shipping. Through long-standing manufacturer contacts and a deep understanding of global supply chains, we work daily to optimise your purchasing prices. Our automated order management system minimises administrative error sources and speeds up processing so that you can maximise your margins in the highly competitive market environment.

Our extended services in detail

  • Exclusive direct partnerships with manufacturers: As a strategic partner of established market leaders, we do not act as a classic middleman, but as an authorised distribution channel. Our cooperations are based on long-term framework agreements, which guarantee continuous availability, market-conform conditions and exclusive access to new product launches. These direct relationships enable transparent supply chains and maximum product authenticity.
  • Complete market conformity and certification: Every product we offer undergoes rigorous compliance checks and is original new goods. All smartphones comply with EU specifications and CE directives and are specially manufactured by the manufacturer for the German economic area.
  • Local warranty processing and after-sales support: The local specialist trade is the primary contact for processing warranty claims of your customers. The devices are subject to the standard manufacturer's warranty, which can be claimed directly on site in the respective country if required.
  • Integrated direct shipping and dropshipping management: Our logistical ecosystem eliminates intermediate storage and reduces capital commitment. Through automated interfaces to leading shipping service providers, we enable just-in-time delivery of your end customers - either under your own brand appearance or as a neutral shipping solution. This scalable model supports both established retailers and growing e-commerce companies.
  • Strategic portfolio and brand management: Our analytics team monitors continuously market trends, price movements and product life cycles. These insights flow into regular portfolio optimisations, with which we strengthen your competitiveness. We identify sales potential for new product categories at an early stage and support you in the market-appropriate positioning of your assortments.

Individual solutions for your business model

We understand that every customer has unique requirements. Therefore, we develop tailor-made solutions that are precisely tailored to your business model, your target group and your growth goals. Whether you want to scale as an established retailer or start as a start-up in e-commerce - we find the optimal partnership structure for your success.

Contact us for a personal consultation on how we can convert your specific requirements into a sustainable competitive strategy.
