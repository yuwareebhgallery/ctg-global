YOUR GLOBAL DIRECT DISTRIBUTION PARTNER

Chumpol Trading Group Co. LTD.

Welcome to the age of efficiency enhancement through direct sourcing from the source without detours or third parties.

In the globally networked mobile communications industry, specialized distributors play a key role in the value chain. Chumpol Trading Group Co. LTD. has been your reliable distribution partner as a trade supplier for the European market with smartphones and electronics for many years.

As a distributor for the DACH region (Germany, Austria and Switzerland), we employ international staff to completely eliminate language barriers and ensure smooth communication.

Thanks to in-depth industry knowledge, many years of experience and a global supplier network, we guarantee you the highest quality, predictability and reliability. Our service portfolio goes far beyond mere procurement:

  • Exclusive direct partnerships: We work exclusively and directly with the original manufacturers and source our smartphones as new goods directly from the production companies on site. As a link between production and trade, we exclusively supply the trade and maintain long-term distributor contracts with the manufacturers for this purpose.
  • Market-compliant goods: All products are intended by the manufacturer for the German/European market, CE-certified, comply with EU specifications and are freely marketable.
  • Local warranty processing: Our smartphones come with the usual manufacturer's warranty, which can be claimed directly on site in Europe in the event of a warranty claim. Warranty processing is carried out in the respective country by authorized specialist retailers.
  • Direct shipping & dropshipping: Without detours and third-party involvement directly from the factory – for maximum margins and consistent prices. On request, we take over fulfillment and shipping directly to your end customers, so you can reduce warehouse costs and expand your service.
  • Portfolio & brand management: Regular product updates and access to the latest market launches secure your competitive advantage. We work closely with Apple, Samsung, Asus, Xiaomi and Huawei and offer you selected top models from these leading manufacturers.
  • Full-service support: Individual advice, transparent processes and personal contacts accompany you from inquiry to delivery.

Through our flexible solutions, we adapt exactly to your requirements, whether you want to order large volumes or purchase smaller contingents of our premium models. With CTG, you optimize your stocks, protect your working capital and focus entirely on growing your business.

Trust in Chumpol Trading Group Co. LTD.: We connect manufacturers and trading partners worldwide and thus create transparent, efficient and cost-effective processes – from the production site to you or your customer.

As a full-service distributor, we guarantee more than hardware. We offer strategic partnerships with SAP support and process design up to market-appropriate positioning. Rely on our expertise for sustainable growth of your sales channels.

Through industry-leading efficiency and innovative sales solutions, we enable our partners to:

  • ► Minimization of operational costs through automated supply chains
  • ► Scalability without warehouse investments (via dropshipping)
  • ► Risk reduction through just-in-time logistics
  • ► Local warranty processing in Europe
  • ► Competitive advantages through exclusive market access

With us you get much more than just products. We are your reliable link between production and trade, you benefit from a global network at home and abroad.

Direct Shipping

Our transport service/distribution is aimed at retailers and wholesalers and is carried out by direct shipping without detours via third-party companies. Trade agreements and an optimized transport route are used to ensure price stability and margin optimization.

Dropshipping

Our dropshipping solutions help you optimize delivery times, warehouse planning and management, rationalize warehouse planning and improve overall administrative efficiency. Dropshipping saves storage and administrative costs.

Portfolio & Warranty

Stay up to date through the manufacturer's distribution. As a partner, you can already help shape the launch of new products in the early phase. All products have full manufacturer's warranty, which can be processed directly on site in Europe in the event of a warranty claim.

Incoterms

The Incoterms "DDP" (Delivered Duty Paid), including goods insurance to the value of the goods, ensure smooth transport of the goods without you having to worry about the import process.

Delivery

Enjoy reliable, fast and timely deliveries throughout Europe, directly to your delivery address. Our reliable air freight service ensures efficient transport and guarantees timely arrival.

Business Relationship

Our partners are the driving force. Benefit from a stable business relationship that offers you a global network, an extensive product range and highest customer satisfaction in a win-win relationship.
