YOUR GLOBAL DIRECT DISTRIBUTION PARTNER

Chumpol Trading Group Co. LTD.

Welcome to the era of increased efficiency through direct sourcing from first hand without detours or third parties.

In the globally connected mobile communications industry, specialized distributors play a key role in the value chain. The Chumpol Trading Group Co. LTD. has been your reliable distribution partner as a trade feeder for the European-wide market with smartphones and electronics for many years.

As a distributor for the DACH region (Germany, Austria and Switzerland), we employ international staff to completely eliminate language barriers and ensure smooth communication.

Thanks to in-depth industry knowledge, many years of experience and a worldwide supplier network, we guarantee you the highest quality, planning reliability and punctuality. Our range of services goes far beyond mere procurement:

  • Exclusive direct partnerships: We work exclusively and directly with the original manufacturers and source our smartphones as new goods directly from the production companies on site. As a link between production and trade, we supply exclusively to the trade and maintain long-term distributor agreements with the manufacturers for this purpose.
  • Market-compliant goods for the DACH region (Germany, Austria, Switzerland): All products are manufactured by the manufacturer exclusively for the German-speaking DACH market, are CE-certified, comply with EU specifications and are freely saleable. Each product is country-specific and has the full manufacturer's warranty in the respective country of sale, which can be claimed at any authorized specialist retailer.
  • Local warranty processing: Our smartphones come with the usual manufacturer's warranty, which can be claimed directly on site in Europe in the event of a warranty claim. Warranty processing is carried out in the respective country by authorized specialist retailers.
  • Direct shipping & dropshipping: Without detours and third-party involvement, directly from the factory – for maximum margins and constant prices. On request, we take over fulfillment and distribution directly to your end customers so that you can reduce storage costs and expand your service. Transport is carried out on a delivery time basis exclusively via DDP (Delivered Duty Paid) including full goods insurance.
  • Portfolio & brand management: Regular product updates and access to the latest market launches give you a competitive advantage. We work closely with Apple, Samsung, Asus, Xiaomi and Huawei and offer you selected top models from these leading manufacturers.
  • Full-service support: Individual advice, transparent processes and personal contacts accompany you from enquiry to delivery.

Our flexible solutions allow us to adapt exactly to your requirements, whether you want to order large volumes or smaller quantities of our premium models. With CTG, you optimize your stocks, conserve your working capital and focus entirely on growing your business.

Trust the Chumpol Trading Group Co. LTD.: We connect manufacturers and trading partners worldwide, creating transparent, efficient and cost-effective processes – from the production facility to you or your customer.

As a full-service distributor, we guarantee more than just hardware. We offer strategic partnerships with SAP support and process design up to market-oriented placement. Rely on our expertise for sustainable growth of your sales channels.

Through industry-leading efficiency and innovative distribution solutions, we enable our partners to:

  • ► Minimize operational costs through automated supply chains
  • ► Scalability without warehouse investment (via dropshipping)
  • ► Risk reduction through just-in-time logistics
  • ► Local warranty processing in Europe
  • ► Competitive advantages through exclusive market access

With us you get far more than just products. We are your reliable link between production and trade, and you benefit from a global network at home and abroad.

Direct Shipping

Our transport service/sales is aimed at individual and wholesale dealers and is carried out through direct shipping without detours via third-party companies. Trade agreements and an optimized transport route are used to ensure price stability and margin optimization.

Dropshipping

Our dropshipping solutions help you optimize delivery times, warehouse planning and management, streamline warehouse planning, and improve overall management efficiency. Dropshipping saves storage and administration costs.

Portfolio & Warranty

Stay up to date through the manufacturer's distribution. As a partner, help shape the introduction of new products at an early stage. All products come with a full manufacturer's warranty that can be processed directly on site in Europe in the event of a claim.

Incoterms

The "DDP" (Delivered Duty Paid) Incoterms, including goods insurance in the amount of the value of the goods, ensure smooth transport of the goods without you having to worry about the import process.

Delivery

Enjoy reliable, fast and punctual deliveries throughout Europe, directly to your delivery address. Our reliable air freight service ensures efficient transport and guarantees on-time arrival.

Business Relationship

Our partners are the driving force. Benefit from a stable business relationship that offers you a global network, a comprehensive product range and the highest level of customer satisfaction in a win-win relationship.
