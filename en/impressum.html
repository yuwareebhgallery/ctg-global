Privacy policy according to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)

The protection of personal data is of the highest priority for us. We process personal data in Germany and the European Union in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. Controller

The controller for the processing of personal data is:
Chumpol Trading Group Co. LTD.

52100 Lampang

2. General information on data processing

We collect and use personal data of our users only insofar as this is necessary to provide a functional website and our content and services. The collection and use of personal data usually only takes place after the user has given their consent. An exception applies in cases where prior consent cannot be obtained for factual reasons and the processing of the data is permitted by legal regulations.

Special categories of personal data: We generally do not process special categories of personal data within the meaning of Art. 9 (1) GDPR (e.g. health data, religious affiliation).

Data sources: If we do not collect data directly from you, it may come from publicly accessible sources (e.g. commercial register) or have been transmitted to us by contractual partners to fulfill an order.

3. Legal basis for processing
  • Art. 6 (1) lit. a GDPR – Consent
  • Art. 6 (1) lit. b GDPR – Performance of a contract or pre-contractual measures
  • Art. 6 (1) lit. c GDPR – Legal obligation
  • Art. 6 (1) lit. f GDPR – Legitimate interest (e.g. security of IT systems, direct advertising for own similar products/services within the scope of existing customer advertising)
4. Type of data processed
  • Master data (name, address)
  • Contact data (telephone number, email address)
  • Contract and business data
  • Payment data
  • Communication data
  • Technical data (IP address, browser, operating system)
5. Purposes of data processing
  • Processing of orders and contracts
  • Processing of inquiries
  • Provision of our services
  • Fulfillment of legal obligations (e.g. commercial and tax retention periods)
  • Ensuring IT security and proper operation of our website
  • Direct advertising for own similar products or services (existing customer advertising), unless you object. You can object at any time (e.g. by email to info@ctg-global.net with the subject "Objection to advertising").
6. Disclosure of data to third parties & recipients

Personal data will only be disclosed if: you have given your explicit consent (Art. 6 (1) lit. a GDPR), this is necessary for the performance of a contract (Art. 6 (1) lit. b GDPR), there is a legal obligation (Art. 6 (1) lit. c GDPR) or a legitimate interest exists and the interests of the data subject do not override (Art. 6 (1) lit. f GDPR).

Specific recipient groups may include: Payment service providers, logistics and shipping service providers, IT service providers and hosting providers, tax advisors, auditors and authorities.

Data transfer to third countries: In the context of international trade and the use of certain service providers, personal data may be transferred to third countries (outside the EU/EEA) without an adequate level of data protection. Such transfers only take place in compliance with Art. 44 et seq. GDPR, in particular on the basis of adequacy decisions by the EU Commission, standard contractual clauses (SCCs) of the EU Commission or other appropriate safeguards. Upon request, we will provide you with the specific safeguards.

7. Cookies, tracking & server log files

Cookies: Our website uses cookies. Necessary cookies (technically required) are based on Art. 6 (1) lit. b or f GDPR. For non-necessary cookies, we obtain your active consent (Art. 6 (1) lit. a GDPR) before they are set.

Server log files: Our hosting provider automatically collects information in so-called server log files (e.g. IP address, date and time of access, browser type, referring website). This data is used for technical security and is deleted after a maximum of 30 days (legal basis: Art. 6 (1) lit. f GDPR).

8. Storage period & deletion

Personal data will only be stored as long as it is necessary to achieve the respective processing purposes or statutory retention periods exist. For contracts and accounting-related data, the statutory periods (6 to 10 years) apply.

9. Rights of data subjects
  • Right of access (Art. 15 GDPR)
  • Right to rectification (Art. 16 GDPR)
  • Right to erasure (Art. 17 GDPR)
  • Right to restriction of processing (Art. 18 GDPR)
  • Right to data portability (Art. 20 GDPR)
  • Right to object (Art. 21 GDPR)
  • Right to withdraw consent (Art. 7 (3) GDPR)

To exercise your rights, please contact: info@ctg-global.net

10. Right to lodge a complaint

You have the right to lodge a complaint with a data protection supervisory authority if you believe that the processing of your personal data violates the GDPR.

11. Data security

We use technical and organizational security measures (TOMs) to protect your data against manipulation, loss, destruction or unauthorized access. This includes in particular the encryption of data transmission via SSL/TLS.

12. Automated decision-making / profiling

We do not use fully automated decision-making within the meaning of Art. 22 GDPR that produces legal effects or similarly significantly affects you.

13. Contacting us

When you contact us by email or telephone, the personal data you provide will only be processed for the purpose of handling your request. The processing is based on Art. 6 (1) lit. b GDPR (pre-contractual measures) or Art. 6 (1) lit. f GDPR (legitimate interest in the efficient processing of requests). The data will be deleted as soon as the processing of the request is complete (usually no later than 6 months thereafter).

14. Non-use of certain services
  • no newsletter distribution,
  • no tracking or analysis tools (e.g. Google Analytics),
  • no social media plugins or embedded social media content,
  • no contact form.
15. Data Protection Officer

A data protection officer has not been appointed because the legal requirements pursuant to Art. 37 GDPR are not met.

16. Data processing of minors

Our website is directed exclusively at adults. We do not knowingly process personal data of children or minors under the age of 16.

17. Changes to this privacy policy

We reserve the right to adapt this privacy policy so that it always complies with current legal requirements or to implement changes to our services in the privacy policy. The current version is always available on our website.
