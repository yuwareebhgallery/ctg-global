OUR SERVICE OVERVIEW

SERVICE AND CAPABILITIES

Our Service – Your strategic advantage in the global market

At Chumpol Trading Group (CTG), we combine state-of-the-art logistics solutions, exclusive product portfolios, and holistic full-service concepts. We see ourselves not just as a supplier, but as a strategic partner for your sustainable growth in the B2B sector. As a pure Asian company headquartered in Thailand, we maintain no branches abroad. Our distribution processes are carried out directly via Trans air Cargo Incoterms DDP.

Direct distribution ex works DDP

Maximum security through our DDP Incoterms (Delivered Duty Paid). We assume full responsibility for the entire flow of goods from the factory. This includes not only transport but also complete handling of all customs formalities, taxes, and full cargo insurance up to your business address. Distribution is carried out on a delivery-time-bound basis and fully insured – more reliable than most domestic logistics partners.

Through our cooperation with global air freight leaders and final last-mile delivery by DHL, we guarantee predictability in a volatile industry.

Exclusive brand partnerships

Gain access to leading brands in the electronics industry. Our global sourcing team ensures daily inventory updates. All products are original goods produced specifically for the respective target markets – no grey market goods.

You get access to the latest models and limited editions – always factory new, in original packaging, and fully CE certified for the respective destination countries.

International support without language barriers

In a globalized economy, we rely on personal contact in your native language. Our international team consists of employees who speak your language. This ensures smooth communication in German, English, Thai, and other relevant business languages. This multilingual expertise ensures that contracts, specifications, and delivery agreements are formulated precisely and implemented legally securely. A dedicated key account manager accompanies you through the entire cycle, from the first sample order to the roll-out of large volume contingents.

Strategic partnerships at the production site

Our unique positioning model is based on a direct presence at the most important production sites of the electronics industry. This proximity to manufacturers enables us not only to have short decision-making paths but also a deep understanding of production processes, quality controls, and supply chains.

As a strategic partner with locations in close proximity to leading production centers, we act as an efficient bridge between manufacturing and distribution. This positioning ensures maximum transparency, direct access to new product launches, and the ability to react quickly to market changes – a decisive competitive advantage in the dynamic electronics industry.

Personal full-service support

Our support concept goes far beyond mere order processing. We offer comprehensive support in all business phases, including:

  • Tracking of shipments with proactive notification of deviations
  • Individual price tiers based on your purchasing volume and market potential
  • After-sales support with dedicated contacts for warranty and service issues
  • Process optimization of your entire supply chain for maximum efficiency

Tailored dropshipping & fulfillment

  • Capital preservation: No high investments in own warehouse staff or space.
  • Speed: Automated processes shorten the time from order to distribution.
  • No overstock or write-offs – you react flexibly to demand and market changes.
  • Correct planning security: Your logistics change from a fixed cost structure to a variable, sales-dependent service.
  • Competitive advantage: Ideal for e-commerce platforms that want to offer a broad range "just-in-time".

Continuous price and process optimization

Our work does not end with shipping. Through long-standing manufacturer contacts and a deep understanding of global supply chains, we work daily to optimize your purchasing prices. Our automated order management system minimizes administrative sources of error and speeds up processing, so you can maximize your margins in a highly competitive market environment.

Our detailed extended services

  • Exclusive direct partnerships with manufacturers: As a strategic partner of established market leaders, we do not act as a classic intermediary but as an authorized sales channel. Our cooperations are based on long-term framework agreements that guarantee continuous availability, market-compliant conditions, and exclusive access to new product launches. These direct relationships enable transparent supply chains and maximum product authenticity.
  • Full market conformity and certification: Every product we offer undergoes rigorous compliance checks and is original new goods. All consumer electronics are manufactured specifically for the respective target markets, comply with EU specifications and CE directives, and are freely marketable. Each product is configured for a specific country and comes with the full manufacturer's warranty in the respective country of sale, which can be claimed at any authorized specialist dealer.
  • Local warranty handling and after-sales support: For the handling of warranty claims of your customers, the local specialist trade is the primary point of contact. The devices are subject to the standard manufacturer's warranty, which can be claimed locally in the respective country if necessary.
  • Integrated direct distribution and dropshipping management: Our logistics ecosystem eliminates intermediate storage and reduces capital commitment. Distribution is carried out exclusively on a delivery-time-bound basis via DDP (Delivered Duty Paid) including full cargo insurance. Through automated interfaces to leading shipping service providers, we enable just-in-time delivery to your end customers – either under your own brand appearance or as a neutral distribution solution. This scalable model supports both established traders and growing e-commerce companies.
  • Strategic portfolio and brand management: Our analytics team continuously monitors market trends, price movements, and product life cycles. These insights flow into regular portfolio optimizations with which we strengthen your competitiveness. We identify early sales potential for new product categories and support you in the market-oriented positioning of your assortments.

Tailored solutions for your business model

We understand that every customer has unique requirements. Therefore, we develop customized solutions that are precisely tailored to your business model, your target group, and your growth goals. Whether you are an established trader looking to scale or a start-up in e-commerce looking to get started – we will find the optimal partnership structure for your success.

Contact us for a personal consultation on how we can transform your specific requirements into a sustainable competitive strategy.

After our start in 2017 as a retail company, our business initially developed successfully. However, the effects of the COVID-19 pandemic led to a temporary slowdown in sales development in the 2021 and 2022 financial years. Since the end of the pandemic-related restrictions, we have continuously expanded our partner network and created the basis for sustainable growth. Today, we are an established company with a strong network and a stable market position. Our company employs 82 people and operates in the B2B environment.

As a purely B2B company, we work exclusively with business partners from our established corporate network. We do not operate an online shop and do not supply private customers. Public online reviews therefore play no role in our business model. This is not an indication of lower trustworthiness. In our industry, cross-company reviews between businesses are not customary.
