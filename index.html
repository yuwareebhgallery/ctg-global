YOUR GLOBAL DIRECT DISTRIBUTION PARTNER

Chumpol Trading Group Co. LTD.

Direct sourcing channels for professional trade – transparent, efficient, and without intermediaries.

In the globally connected electronics industry, specialized suppliers play a key role in the value chain. Chumpol Trading Group Co. LTD. has been your reliable supply partner as a trade feeder for the European market with consumer electronics and household electronics for many years.

As a pure Asian company headquartered in Thailand, we maintain no branches or offices outside Thailand. Our distribution processes are carried out directly from the factory via Trans air Cargo Incoterms DDP. We supply exclusively commercial customers and trading partners – no private individuals.

Thanks to deep industry expertise, many years of experience, and an established supply network, we guarantee the highest quality, reliability, and adherence to schedules. Our range of services goes far beyond mere procurement:

  • Exclusive direct partnerships with manufacturers: We work exclusively and directly with the original manufacturers and source our consumer electronics as new goods directly from the production companies on site. As a link between production and trade, we supply only the trade sector and maintain long-term supply partner agreements with the manufacturers.
  • Country-specific and market-determined goods: All products are intended by the manufacturer for the respective target countries, CE-certified, comply with EU specifications, and are freely marketable. Each product is configured for a specific country and comes with the full manufacturer's warranty in the respective country of sale, which can be claimed at any authorized specialist dealer.
  • Local warranty handling: Our products carry the usual manufacturer's warranty, which can be claimed locally in Europe in the event of a warranty claim. Warranty handling is carried out in the respective country by authorized specialist dealers.
  • Direct distribution & dropshipping: Without detours and third-party involvement, directly from the factory – for maximum margins and stable prices. Upon request, we take over fulfillment and distribution directly to your end customers, so you can reduce storage costs and expand your service. Distribution is carried out on a delivery-time-bound basis exclusively via DDP (Delivered Duty Paid) including full cargo insurance.
  • Portfolio & brand management: Regular product updates and access to the latest market launches give you a competitive edge. We work closely with leading manufacturers and offer you selected top models from these manufacturers.
  • Full-service support: Individual advice, transparent processes, and personal contact persons accompany you from the inquiry to delivery.

With our flexible solutions, we adapt precisely to your requirements, whether you want to order large volumes or smaller contingents of our premium models. With CTG, you optimize your inventory, conserve your working capital, and focus entirely on growing your business.

Trust Chumpol Trading Group Co. LTD.: We connect manufacturers and trading partners worldwide, creating transparent, efficient, and cost-effective processes – from the production facility to you or your customer.

As a full-service supply partner, we guarantee more than just hardware. We offer strategic partnerships with SAP support and process design up to market-ready placement. Trust our expertise for the sustainable growth of your sales channels.

Through industry-leading efficiency and innovative distribution solutions, we enable our partners to:

  • ► Minimize operating costs through automated supply chains
  • ► Achieve scalability without warehouse investment (via dropshipping)
  • ► Reduce risk through just-in-time logistics
  • ► Benefit from local warranty handling in Europe
  • ► Gain competitive advantages through exclusive market access

After our start in 2017 as a retail company, our business initially developed successfully. However, the effects of the COVID-19 pandemic led to a temporary slowdown in sales development in the 2021 and 2022 financial years. Since the end of the pandemic-related restrictions, we have continuously expanded our partner network and created the basis for sustainable growth. Today, we are an established company with a strong network and a stable market position. Our company employs 82 people and operates in the B2B environment.

As a purely B2B company, we work exclusively with business partners from our established corporate network. We do not operate an online shop and do not supply private customers. Public online reviews therefore play no role in our business model. This is not an indication of lower trustworthiness. In our industry, cross-company reviews between businesses are not customary.

With us, you get much more than just products. We are your reliable link between production and trade, and you benefit from a global network at home and abroad.

Direct Distribution

Our distribution processes are aimed at retailers and wholesalers and are carried out through direct distribution without detours via third-party companies. Trade agreements and an optimized transport route are used to ensure price stability and margin optimization.

Dropshipping

Our dropshipping solutions help you optimize distribution times, warehouse planning and management, streamline inventory planning, and improve overall administrative efficiency. Dropshipping saves storage and administrative costs.

Portfolio & Warranty

Stay up to date through manufacturer distribution. As a partner, help shape the launch of new products at an early stage. All products come with a full manufacturer's warranty that can be handled locally in Europe in the event of a claim.

Incoterms

The Incoterms "DDP" (Delivered Duty Paid), including cargo insurance up to the value of the goods, ensure smooth transport of the goods without you having to worry about the import process.

Distribution

Benefit from reliable, fast and punctual distribution throughout Europe, directly to your business address. Our reliable air freight service ensures efficient transport and guarantees on-time arrival.

Business Relationship

Our partners are the driving force. Benefit from a stable business relationship that offers you a global network, an extensive product range and the highest level of customer satisfaction in a win-win relationship.
